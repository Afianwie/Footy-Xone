Should Manchester City Sign Dani Olmo?

Published on 7 June 2025 / Transfer Watch

Dani Olmo has quietly grown into one of Europe’s most versatile attacking midfielders. With injuries behind him and a productive year at RB Leipzig, many believe he’s ready for a move to an elite club.

Manchester City, known for their technical midfielders, could benefit from Olmo’s intelligence and positional awareness. He fits Guardiola’s mold — creative, composed, and comfortable in tight spaces.

But with Foden, De Bruyne, and Bernardo already in the mix, would Olmo get enough minutes? City must weigh the need for depth against the danger of overstocking. Still, his quality is undeniable.
