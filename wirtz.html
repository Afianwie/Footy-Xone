Why is There Too Much Transfer Saga Behind Wirtz?

Published on 7 June 2025 / Bayer Leverkusen

Florian Wirtz has become one of the hottest properties in European football. After a stellar season with Bayer Leverkusen, where he was instrumental in their domestic and European campaigns, top clubs have been circling relentlessly.

What makes the Wirtz saga so complicated is not just his price tag, but his commitment to Leverkusen’s project and the influence of his father-agent. Bayern, Real Madrid, and Manchester City are all reportedly interested, but Wirtz has not pushed for a move — fueling speculation and fan anxiety.

As the summer unfolds, this saga may turn into a battle of patience and negotiation. One thing is certain — Wirtz won’t come cheap, and he won't be easy to convince either.
